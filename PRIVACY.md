When you use our service, you may provide:

### Automatically Collected Information

We may collect:

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:
- Process your PDF files with the details you enter
- Improve our service functionality

## Data Storage and Processing

- **No Permanent Storage**: Your uploaded PDF files and personal details are processed in memory and are NOT permanently stored on our servers

- **No Database**: We do not maintain a database of user information or uploaded files

We do NOT:

We MAY share information only:
