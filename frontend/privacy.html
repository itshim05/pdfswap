Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Auto-Lab Personalizer. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary for the operation of our service:

  • Uploaded Files: PDF files you upload are processed temporarily on our servers to apply your requested changes. They are automatically deleted immediately after processing or within 10 minutes.
  • Form Data: Information you enter (Name, Roll No, etc.) is used solely to personalize your PDF files and is not stored permanently.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., number of files processed) to improve our service.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide the PDF personalization service you requested.
  • Improve our website and user experience.
  • Display non-personalized advertisements via Google AdSense.

4. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. Your files are processed in a secure environment and are not accessible to other users.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the support channels provided on our website.
